About

Kai Pörtfors, better known by his stage name Kuya Kai is a half Filipino and half Finnish Deejay and is currently living in Helsinki, Finland. He is known to make the dancefloor go crazy with his mixes and sick vibes. He plays a wide range genres such as Hip Hop, House, Funk and the list goes on. He is currently the Resident DJ of the legendary PerjantaiBonk (LeBonk, Helsinki). If you ever get the chance to see him perform live, don't miss out!
